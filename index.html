What's Hip Pop Culture

嘻哈文化誕生於 1970 年代的紐約布朗克斯，如今已跨越國界成為全球共通的語言。饒舌、DJ、街舞與塗鴉藝術交織出今日的嘻哈文化，影響著無數人的生命」

Born in the Bronx during the 1970s as a creative response to social marginalization, hip-hop culture is a global phenomenon anchored by four foundational pillars: MCing, DJing, Breaking, and Graffiti Art.
